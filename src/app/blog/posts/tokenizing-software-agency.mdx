## The Problem with Traditional Agencies

Software agencies are typically black boxes. Clients pay invoices without seeing the work. Employees grind without equity. The community has no stake in success.

I wanted to flip this model entirely.

## Enter $BEARCO

$BEARCO isn't just a meme token — it's an experiment in radical transparency. Every line of code I write for BearifiedCo is streamed live 24/7 on pump.fun. Anyone can watch. Anyone can verify the work is happening.

## Why Pump.fun?

Pump.fun's livestream feature is perfect for this experiment. It combines:

1. **Token launch mechanics** — fair launch, community-driven
2. **Live content** — 24/7 coding stream
3. **Community engagement** — real-time interaction

Most tokens on pump.fun are pure speculation. I wanted to prove you can build real products while streaming.

Each product uses BEARCO tokens, creating utility beyond speculation.

- More transparent than traditional agencies
